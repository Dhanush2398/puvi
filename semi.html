Semi-Permanent Makeup – Enhance Your Natural Beauty Effortlessly

Semi-permanent makeup is a modern beauty solution designed to enhance your natural features with long-lasting results. At Puvi, we offer safe and professional treatments that help you achieve perfectly shaped brows, defined lips, and enhanced eyes — without the need for daily makeup. Our approach focuses on natural-looking results that complement your unique facial features.

Using advanced techniques and high-quality pigments, we ensure precision, hygiene, and comfort throughout the procedure. Our Semi-Permanent Makeup Services

At Puvi, we specialize in enhancing your natural beauty through safe and professional semi-permanent makeup treatments. Our goal is not just to apply makeup, but to create long-lasting, natural-looking results that suit your face, skin tone, and personal style. Every treatment is performed with precision, hygiene, and expert care to ensure comfort and confidence.

01.
Microblading & Ombre Brows

Get perfectly shaped, fuller-looking eyebrows with natural strokes or soft shaded effects that enhance your facial features.

02.
Lip Blush & Tint

Achieve naturally tinted, fuller lips with improved color, symmetry, and definition for a fresh and youthful look.

03.
Eyeliner Enhancement

Define your eyes with subtle or bold eyeliner that enhances your natural eye shape without daily application.

04.
Micropigmentation

Advanced technique that deposits pigment into the skin to enhance facial features, restore color, and create natural-looking results.

05.
Pigmentation Correction

Corrects uneven tones and discoloration to achieve a more balanced, even, and natural skin appearance.

06.
Lip Pigmentation Correction

Targets dark or uneven lip tones to restore natural color and improve lip brightness and overall appearance.

07.
Lip Neutralization

Balances darker lip tones using corrective pigments to create a more even base before achieving the desired lip color.

08.
BB / CC Glow Treatment

A semi-permanent skin treatment that provides a radiant glow, evens skin tone, and gives a natural foundation-like finish.
