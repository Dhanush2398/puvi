Soap Making Workshop

Join our professional Soap Making Workshop at Puvi and learn the art of creating natural, handmade cold process soaps. This workshop is designed for beginners and skincare enthusiasts who want to explore natural formulations and start their own skincare journey.

Our hands-on training covers everything from ingredients selection to soap formulation, safety measures, and curing techniques. You will gain practical knowledge and confidence to create your own customized soaps.

What You Will Learn

This workshop provides complete guidance on soap making using natural ingredients and Ayurvedic techniques.

  • Cold Process Soap Making Basics
  • Understanding Oils, Butters & Lye
  • Natural Coloring & Fragrance Techniques
  • Safety & Handling Procedures
  • Soap Curing & Storage Methods

By the end of the workshop, you will be able to create your own handmade soaps and understand how to build a skincare brand using natural products.

Who Can Join

This workshop is perfect for beginners, skincare lovers, entrepreneurs, and anyone interested in learning natural soap making.

  • Beginners & Students
  • Homemakers & Hobbyists
  • Skincare Enthusiasts
  • Aspiring Entrepreneurs
